Welcome to my webpage

Hi, I am Vikas Chauhan. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the National Taipei University of Technology Taipei Taiwan. Here I am working in the Intelligent Control Lab with Prof. Leehter Yao. I have completed my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Aruna Tiwari from the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore India. In particular, my Ph.D. dissertation work has focused on proposing multilabel approaches using noniterative and deep learning algorithms. I specialize in the field of Artificial Intelligence, with a particular focus on machine learning and deep learning. My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and deep learning. I am interested in exploring innovative algorithms to solve real-world problems. If you are interested in collaborating with me or learning more about my research, please get in touch.
